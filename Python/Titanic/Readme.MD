## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Titanic passenger dataset to uncover patterns in survival rates across demographics, passenger class, cabin deck, and embarkation port.

The analysis is structured around five key questions:

1. Who were the passengers on the Titanic?
2. What deck were the passengers on and how does that relate to their class?
3. Where did the passengers come from?
4. Who was alone and who was with family?
5. What factors helped someone survive the sinking?

The dataset contains **891 records** and **12 features** including passenger class, sex, age, fare, cabin, and survival status.
